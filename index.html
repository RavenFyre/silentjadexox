A bit about me

Hello! I'm Jade and I am a self-taught programmer. My love for programming started with building databases and Discord bots in Python and Java, and more recently I have been learning website development in HTML, CSS & Javascript, with the intention of building and designing a web interface to serve as a dashboard to manage my Discord bots. I have a keen interest in developing my own skills further in the creation of Discord bots, websites, databases and applications. I am also capable of using Git & Github, including the command line terminal, push/pull requests and handling merge conflicts, and while I prefer solo-working as that is within my comfort zone, I am excited to join a team of developers and collaboratively help with building the future of FIS.
